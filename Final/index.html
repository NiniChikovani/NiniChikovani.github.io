CO2 Emissions Data Visualization

Final Project for ARTG 5300

There are two datasets compared on this small multiples chart. Data n.1 is CO2 emission per capita,
while data n.2 is annual CO2 emissions. There will be multiple layers of contextual information analised
in this visualization - such as the population and the current GDP of each country. The chart depicts 10 countries.
They were chosen according to the population - above 5 million. Data about the GDP and population was gathered
from the World Bank website, while the data about C02 emissions was gathered from the Our World in Data page.
